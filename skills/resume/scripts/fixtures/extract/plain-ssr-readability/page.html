Senior Platform Engineer

Example Corp is hiring a Senior Platform Engineer to lead the next chapter of our infrastructure platform. You will own the engineering roadmap for internal tooling used by hundreds of engineers across the company.

What you will own

  • Kubernetes platform design, rollout, and operations across multiple clouds
  • Terraform modules and the internal developer-platform portal
  • Observability, SLOs, and incident response practices
  • Security posture and compliance for our production infrastructure

What we are looking for

  • 7+ years of platform or infrastructure engineering experience
  • Deep knowledge of Kubernetes, Terraform, and at least one major cloud (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
  • A track record of leading cross-org technical initiatives
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
