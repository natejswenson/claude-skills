A 75% success rate on your agent sounds shippable. Run it three times in a row and the odds all three pass drop to 42%.

That gap is the number most agent evals miss. Measuring "did it work at least once" flatters you; what a user actually feels is whether it works every single time.

For anything customer-facing, average accuracy is the wrong target. Measure how often it succeeds on every attempt, and optimize that.
